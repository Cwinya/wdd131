Uganda is a land-locked country located in East Africa. It is known for its diverse wildlife, beautiful landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. It is bordered by Kenya to the east, South Sudan to the north, the Democratic Republic of the Congo to the west, and Rwanda and Tanzania to the south. It has a population of over 45 million people and is home to several ethnic groups, each with its own unique traditions and customs. There are above 40 tribes in Uganda. There are many national parks and wildlife reserves in Uganda, making it a popular destination for eco-tourism.

Site Plan for Global Gastronome

Site Name

Global Gastronome

This name was chosen to reflect the site's focus on food from diverse cultures around the world. "Gastronome" implies a lover and connoisseur of good food, suggesting high-quality, authentic recipes. "Global" highlights the international scope, which is central to the project's theme.

Optional domain availability: globalgastronome.com

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for exploring global cuisines by offering authentic recipes from various cultures, cultural insights into food traditions, and a community platform for sharing personal recipes and experiences.

Scenarios

  • What are some traditional recipes from Italy?
  • How can I share my own family recipe on the site?

Color Schema

Forest Green (#228B22): Used for headings and accents to represent freshness and nature in food.

Orange (#FFA500): Used for backgrounds and highlights to evoke warmth and the vibrancy of global flavors.

Typography

Georgia: Used for headings to provide a classic, elegant feel.

Arial: Used for body text for readability and modernity.
